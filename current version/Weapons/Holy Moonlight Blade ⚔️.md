# Holy Moonlight Blade ⚔️

_Its said that, at the end, this sword led Ludwig back to humanity one last time_

Radiant Slash  
1–10: Deal 2 damage
11–20: Deal 3 damage
If your Beasthood is 1 or less, heal 1 HP.

Moonlight Arc  
1–15: Deal x damage to two enemies  
16–20: Deal x damage to three enemies  
where x is two minus your beasthood
Gain 1 beasthood

**Mastery:**  
End a combat encounter with 0 Beasthood after using Moonlight Arc at least two times in that combat.  
_Unlocks:_ 

Ludwig’s Redemption  
Can only be used if your Beasthood is 0.  
1–10: Deal 5 damage to one enemy, 3 to another, and 1 to another 
11-20: Deal 7 damage to one enemy, 5 to another, and 3 to another
Heal 2 hp  
Gain 3 Beasthood.
