# Bowblade

_An elegant sword which splits to form a silver bow, crafted specfically for Simon, who scorned the other church hunters increasing reliance on gunpowder. But aside from a few close friends, Simon was scoffed at for his choice of arms, for who would dare face the beasts with a measly bow?_

Harrowed Swipe:
1-12: Deal 2 damage
13-20: Deal 3 damage 

Bloodtinge:
Gain 1 beasthood.  
Place 1 arrow on this card.  
You may lose any amount of health; for every 2 health lost, place 1 additional arrow on this card.  
This effect does not cost an action and may only be used once per turn.


**Mastery:**  
Have 5 or more arrows on this card
